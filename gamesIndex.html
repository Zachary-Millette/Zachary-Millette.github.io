Enemy AI Programmer, 2017

A single player, third person, fast paced, survival shooter. As a lone survivor waiting to be extracted to safety after sea creatures threaten to take the surface, dodge, warp, and battle to survive.

This project was my first attempt at enemy AI and has expanded my understanding and knowledge on the subject. One aspect about the project that went exceedingly well was the team's ability to maintain a realistic schedule and not over-scope the project. I still wish we could implement more of the ideas we had into the game. Going forward from this project I better understand the importance of documentation such as the GDD, TDD, and ASG.

Made in Unreal Engine 4
Available on itch.io: Project RedTide

Lead Programmer, 2017

An action game in an isometric perspective where the player controls a rag-doll navigating through the world dodging explosives and hazards while interacting with objects. Face the challenge alone or with a friend.

The shortest time frame I have ever had in creating a game was the twelve weeks my team and I had to make Project Backfire. This game was complete fun madness in one package. As a lead programmer I was responsible for creating the behavior of the explosives in the game and the systems that drop them.

Made in Unreal Engine 4
Available on itch.io: Project Backfire

Lead Programmer and Lead Designer, 2017

A match three game in a top-down perspective. Navigate through the nightmare as you seek the comfort of your bed.

This game was a great joy to make. Creating what I believe to be a very unique take on the match three genre was very challenging, exciting, and rewarding. Players control characters that move around the screen breaking away from the tradition of directly moving match pieces. Looking back I have learned not to creatively limit myself if I have not found a game that has done something similar. With the right team any creative endeavor is possible.

Made in Unity 5
Available on itch.io: Night Terrors

Graphic Designer, 2016

A one-v-one top-down twin-stick shooter. Similar to Rock, Paper, Scissors players choose between three shapes and battle till they defeat the other player. Enter zones to change shape and gain the advantage.

This video game was originally created for Ludum Dare 35 by a group of talented individuals I was fortunate enough to work with. After having gained more experience in creating video games I decided to revisit the project and see if I could recreate it. I am very satisfied with the results and hope it is as fun to play as it was to make. I have included links to both the original and remade versions of the game.

Game Designer, Fall 2016

A whimsical board game based on the Grimm fairy tale The Fox and the Cat. Fallacious Feline is a cooperative/competitive card-flipping game where up to five players (foxes) compete against a sixth player (the cat) who is trying to prevent them from reaching the top of the game board (the tree) through the use of different types of cards that have different effects when flipped.
